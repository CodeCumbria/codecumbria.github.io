About

Cumbria's first group for programmers and digital creatives.

  • We meet on the last Wednesday of every month in Carlisle City Centre
  • Host various talks from within the group
  • Work on community based projects
  • Are committed to the development of IT within Cumbria
  • Our group ranges from Students to the professional
  • We cover a multitude of programming languages
  • We are a friendly informal group and welcome new members readily
