Code Cumbria is an inclusive group for anyone involved or interested in technology, software development, or digital creativity in Cumbria, North-West England, & South-West Scotland.

We currently meet regularly on the last Wednesday of every month, and are in the process of setting up additional events.

Subscribing to our newsletter is the best way to stay informed about our events, and you can also find us on Twitter, Facebook, Google+ and YouTube.
