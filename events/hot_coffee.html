The next {{ page.title }} will take place at The Coffee Kitchen in Cockermouth on Tuesday October 15th, 2013 beginning at 11:00 and ending at 14:00.

{{ page.title }} is a new co-working event in the Code Cumbria calendar, and aims to help those of us that work from home break the cycle of not leaving the house for days at a time or working in our pyjamas!

There's no pattern yet to the events, but the intention is to meet in a different coffee shop (in a different town) at least once a month. We're starting in Cockermouth, but will be including Carlisle, Penrith, Keswick, Workington and Whitehaven in the rotation.

The emphasis is on getting work done, and not just socialising - we just happen to be working in a different place (with people!).

No ticket/RSVP is required - just turn up!
