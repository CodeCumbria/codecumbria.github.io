The next {{ page.title }} will take place on Wednesday February 19th, 2014 at The Coffee Kitchen in Cockermouth. People will be around from 10AM onwards - we will let the staff know we are there, but look for a table full of laptops!

{{ page.title }} is a new co-working event in the Code Cumbria calendar, and aims to help those of us that work from home break the cycle of not leaving the house for days at a time or working in our pyjamas!

There's no pattern yet to the events, but the intention is to meet in a different coffee shop (in a different town) at least once a month. We started in Cockermouth in October 2013, but will hopefully be including Carlisle, Penrith, Keswick, Workington and Whitehaven in the rotation.

The emphasis is on getting work done, and not just socialising - we just happen to be working in a different place (with people!).

No ticket/RSVP is required - just turn up! (but do tweet us if you're coming, so we know who to look out for)
